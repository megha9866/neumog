# Neumog Product Story

## One-Sentence Positioning

Neumog builds and runs digital products using a hybrid model: a tight internal leadership core plus a vetted network of senior freelancers and, soon, RLHF/data-annotation experts.

## Core Proof Points

- **End-to-end accountability**: strategy, design, build, launch, and runâ€”not single tickets.
- **Hybrid delivery model**: internal product, delivery, and architecture leads orchestrate specialist freelancers from MNCs/product orgs.
- **Industry focus**: e-commerce, hospitality, food/grocery delivery, local service marketplaces, plus near-future RLHF & data annotation programs.
- **Managed partner, not a gig marketplace**: curated teams, shared rituals, SLAs, and platform visibility distinguish Neumog from Upwork/Fiverr-style marketplaces.

## Audiences & Desired Outcomes

| Audience | Needs | Key Messages | Primary CTA |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Clients (founders, PMs, CTOs) | Reliable product delivery without maintaining a huge bench | Hybrid teams, transparent execution, faster ramp | `Start a project`, `Talk to our team` |
| Freelance experts | Serious projects, curated teams, fair pay, flexible load | Work with internal leads, enterprise-grade delivery, no admin drag | `Apply as an expert` |
| Future RLHF / annotation specialists | Early access to future platform | Neumog expanding into data workstreams, paid per task | `Join the waitlist` |

## Narrative Pillars

1. **Hybrid product squads**: describe how internal leads + external experts co-build, including matching, rituals, QA.
2. **Domain accelerators**: show pre-built knowledge and accelerators for target verticals.
3. **Transparent delivery platform**: even before portals launch, promise dashboards, analytics, and lead capture.
4. **Future-proof talent network**: highlight path to RLHF/data annotation to attract talent early.

## Voice & Tone

- Confident, enterprise-ready, but modern and personable.
- Use verbs like *ship*, *orchestrate*, *assemble*, *accelerate*.
- Balance visionary statements with concrete stats/testimonials.

## Content Hooks for Pages

- **Home**: big promise + proof (stats, testimonials, hybrid explanation).
- **How it Works**: step-by-step process, inside look at orchestration.
- **Services**: vertical deep dives, upcoming RLHF program.
- **Experts**: benefits, onboarding process, culture proof.
- **Case Studies**: social proof placeholder until real stories.
- **Blog**: thought leadership on hybrid delivery, domain insights, data work.
- **Contact / Start Project**: high-intent capture with reassurance on approach times.
